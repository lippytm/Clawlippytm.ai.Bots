# Project Role

## Purpose
`Clawlippytm.ai.Bots` is the public bot product and conversational systems hub within the wider lippytm ecosystem.

## Primary Objectives
- present bot concepts and use cases
- document conversational AI product ideas
- support public-facing bot identity and demos
- connect public bot experiences to internal systems

## Inputs
- bot ideas
- conversational flows
- public use cases
- feature concepts
- ecosystem integrations

## Outputs
- product documentation
- use-case descriptions
- public bot architecture notes
- demo guidance
- cross-links to the control tower and MyClaw ops

## Connected Repositories
- `lippytm.ai`
- `MyClaw.lippytm.AI-`
- `lippytm-lippytm.ai-tower-control-ai`

## KPIs
- clarity of bot purpose
- demo usefulness
- product positioning quality
- integration alignment
